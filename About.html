ByteWrench Create is a small catalog of soldering tools, dev boards, and hands-on kits for makers, hobbyist engineers, and anyone learning electronics by actually building something.

We stock a mix of beginner kits (no soldering experience required), intermediate tools for people leveling up their bench, and advanced gear — RFID research tools, programmable power supplies, radio hardware — for people who already know exactly what they need.

Every product is picked for a reason: it either solves a real problem on the bench, teaches something worth knowing, or is just a genuinely good piece of hardware worth having around.

What we believe

Good tools should be honest about what they do. We'd rather sell you the right $9 sensor than the wrong $90 one.
